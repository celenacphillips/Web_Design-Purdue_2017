The recipes Lilly has created or modified have a history of their own. Originally, she was just taking recipes other people had created and making them. However, often she would find the taste wasn't that good. She had a thought to start modifying recipes.

First, she started with bariatric-safe recipes, modifying them to taste good, as good or better than normal foods. Then she moved to the more complex step: taking normal food recipes and modifying/ substituting for bariatric-safe ingredients.

Her list of recipes is quite lengthy, most of which you cannot tell the difference between the modified and original foods.
