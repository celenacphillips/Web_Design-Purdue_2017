Lilly always had a love for food. She took a food safety course in her 30s so she could open her own restaurant. Life had other plans for Lilly at the time, but she eventually made her dream happen with Mommy Business.

Nowadays, making food is a sort of meditation for Lilly. Whenever she's stressed, you can always find her in the kitchen, creating and modifying new recipes and having fun. That is the heart of Mommy Business: the love and passion from one woman.
